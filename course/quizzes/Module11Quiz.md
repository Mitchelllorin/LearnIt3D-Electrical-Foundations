## Module 11 Quiz — Motors, Loads & Applications

**Instructions:** Answer all 10 questions. Show working for calculation questions and use nameplate-style reasoning where applicable.

## Questions

1. **Multiple Choice**  
   An AC induction motor develops torque because AC in the stator creates a:
   - A. Fixed chemical field
   - B. Rotating magnetic field
   - C. Constant DC polarity
   - D. Neutral-only circuit

2. **Fill in the Blank**  
   The difference between synchronous speed and actual rotor speed is called **__________**.

3. **Multiple Choice**  
   Which is a major advantage of three-phase motors?
   - A. They are never used above 120 V
   - B. They are self-starting and provide smoother torque
   - C. They do not require overload protection
   - D. They operate only on DC

4. **Short Calculation**  
   A motor has a full-load current of **12 A**. Using the module rule of thumb `starting current ≈ 6 × FLA`, estimate the starting current.

5. **True / False**  
   An overload relay is intended to help protect motor windings from sustained overcurrent and overheating.

6. **Multiple Choice**  
   Which starting method applies full line voltage to the motor immediately?
   - A. DOL
   - B. Soft starter only
   - C. VFD speed trim mode only
   - D. Isolation transformer mode

7. **Fill in the Blank**  
   A Canadian motor nameplate for standard utility supply commonly lists a frequency of **__________ Hz**.

8. **Multiple Choice**  
   Motors are primarily what type of electrical load?
   - A. Resistive
   - B. Inductive
   - C. Purely capacitive
   - D. Electrostatic only

9. **Short Calculation**  
   A motor takes in **4.0 kW** of electrical power and delivers **3.4 kW** of mechanical output. Calculate the efficiency.

10. **True / False**  
    A VFD can vary motor speed by controlling frequency and voltage.

## Answer Key

1. **B** — The rotating magnetic field is what produces induction and torque.  
2. **Slip**  
3. **B** — Three-phase motors are self-starting and provide smoother torque.  
4. **72 A** — `6 × 12`.  
5. **True**  
6. **A** — DOL applies full voltage immediately.  
7. **60**  
8. **B** — Motors are mainly inductive loads.  
9. **85%** — `3.4 / 4.0 × 100`.  
10. **True**

## Module 4 Quiz — Electrical Power & Energy

**Instructions:** Answer all 10 questions. Show working for calculation questions and state final units.

## Questions

1. **Short Calculation**  
   A 120 V kettle draws 12 A. Calculate its power in watts.

2. **Multiple Choice**  
   Electric utilities normally bill energy use in:
   - A. Watts
   - B. Volt-amperes
   - C. Kilowatt-hours
   - D. Ohms per hour

3. **True / False**  
   A 100 W lamp running for 10 hours uses 1 kWh of energy.

4. **Short Calculation**  
   A 240 V heater has a resistance of 24 Ω. Calculate its power using `P = V² / R`.

5. **Fill in the Blank**  
   The basic energy relationship is `E = __________`.

6. **Multiple Choice**  
   Power factor is defined as:
   - A. `S / P`
   - B. `P / S`
   - C. `V / R`
   - D. `I / V`

7. **Short Calculation**  
   Electricity costs `$0.15/kWh`. What is the cost of using `6 kWh`?

8. **True / False**  
   A 1500 W heater on 120 V draws about 12.5 A.

9. **Multiple Choice**  
   Efficiency is best described as:
   - A. Output power divided by input power
   - B. Input power divided by output current
   - C. Voltage divided by power factor
   - D. Apparent power minus real power

10. **Fill in the Blank**  
    The unit commonly used for apparent power is **__________**.

## Answer Key

1. **1440 W** — `P = V × I = 120 × 12`.  
2. **C** — Utilities bill in kWh.  
3. **True** — `100 W × 10 h = 1000 Wh = 1 kWh`.  
4. **2400 W** — `P = 240² / 24`.  
5. **P × t**  
6. **B** — `PF = P / S`.  
7. **$0.90** — `6 × 0.15`.  
8. **True** — `I = P / V = 1500 / 120 = 12.5 A`.  
9. **A**  
10. **VA**
